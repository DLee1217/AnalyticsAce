Buehler Still Perfect in World Series

The Dodgers have now taken a 3-0 series lead against the Yankees. The Dodgers were once again led by a Freddie Freeman home run, and great pitching managed to clinch the decisive lead.

Walker Buehler managed to continue his run of dominance in the World Series, with another commanding win for the Dodgers. He had a DL+ of 127 due to his run prevention and good peripherals. Walker Buehler put the Dodgers in an amazing position, as teams that have gone up 3-0 in the World Series have won every time.

The Dodgers plan to put out a bullpen game, a strategy that has been up and down this October.
