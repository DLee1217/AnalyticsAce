Guardians Ride Strong Bullpen and Explosive Offense to Reach the ALCS

The Guardians, after staving off elimination by the Tigers and forcing a Game 5, rode a dominant bullpen and powerful offense to their way to the ALCS.

Matthew Boyd started the game strong for the Guardians pitching 2 strong innings with 5 strikeouts. He is our Ace of the Day, as the opposing starter, Tarik Skubal, had a rare bad game. Boyd had a 134 DL, a new record in the stat.

Lane Thomas had a grand slam for the Guardians, and Steven Kwan, Andres Gimenez, and Brayan Rocchio had multi-hit games.

Boyd was followed by Cade Smith, who pitched another 1.2 innings of scoreless ball. Erik Sabrowski and Andrew Walters also pitched 0.1 innings each out of relief. Tim Herrin, Hunter Gaddis. and Eli Morgan completed through 7 innings, and Emmanuel Clase came in for the 6 out save.

The LCS brackets are now complete, as we know that the Yankees will face off against the Guardians, and the Mets will face off against the Dodgers.

Thank you for reading, and see you again on tomorrow's edition of Ace of the Day!
