Carlos Rodon Dominant in Game 1 Victory for the Yankees

After steamrolling through the Royals in the ALDS, the Yankees continue their dominance in the postseason with a Game 1 victory over the Kansas City Royals.

Although certain offensive bats, like Aaron Judge, are yet to wake up, Carlos Rodon did not miss a beat in his latest start. He tied the record for the highest single game DL with 134, a feat that Matthew Boyd accomplished two days ago.
