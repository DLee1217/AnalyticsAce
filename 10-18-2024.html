Luis Gil Dominant in his First Postseason Start

Coming off of a breakout 2024 campaign, the rookie Luis Gil continues the Yankees' string of impressive starting pitching to take a decisive 3-1 lead in the series.

Gil threw 4 innings of 2 run ball, surrendering no home runs and 3 hits. He received a DL+ of 123, meaning that he was 23% above average. He induced soft contact, even though he let up more runs that desired.

The Yankees now look to eliminate the Guardians in a decisive Game 5 on Saturday.
